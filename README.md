### 1. Energy-Information-Coordination Framework (Short, ~1200 words)

### 2. Goodhart's Law in Product Design (Medium, ~2300 words)
**File**: [goodharts-law-product-design.md](goodharts-law-product-design.md)

**Core insight**: When metrics become targets, they cease to be good measures.

Covers:
- How A/B testing optimizes for exploitation at the margin
- Real examples: Facebook News Feed, YouTube recommendations, e-commerce dark patterns
- Business model dependency (ads ‚Üí manipulation, subscription ‚Üí alignment)
- Mitigation strategies: Multi-metric scorecards, trust KPIs, red-teaming

**Use case**: For product builders who want to avoid Goodhart traps

---

### 3. AI Power Concentration (Long, ~3500 words)
**File**: [ai-power-concentration.md](ai-power-concentration.md)

**Core paradox**: Intelligence abundance ‚â† power diffusion when infrastructure is scarce.

Comprehensive treatment of:
- Capital intensity creating natural monopolies ($100M+ for GPT-4 training)
- Prisoner's dilemma in AI race (why coordination is harder than nuclear weapons)
- Substrate control: Energy > Compute > Materials (physical chokepoints)
- Two-tier reality scenario: Public AI vs. elite airgapped models
- Three futures: Oligopoly lock-in, orphaned superintelligence, or coordination breakthrough

**Use case**: Strategic analysis for policymakers, researchers, investors

## üé® Content Philosophy

### Core Principles:
1. **Systems thinking over blame**: Analyze incentives, not individuals
2. **Historical context**: Patterns repeat, substrate changes
3. **Actionable insights**: Theory ‚Üí practice, always include "so what?"
4. **Steel-man counterarguments**: Critique strongest form of opposing views
5. **Cross-domain pattern recognition**: UX dark patterns = political demagoguery = institutional capture (same mechanics)

### Writing Guidelines:
- **Short articles (800-1500 words)**: Single clear insight, shareable
- **Medium articles (1500-2500 words)**: Explore concept with depth, multiple angles
- **Long articles (2500-4000 words)**: Comprehensive treatment, reference material

### Cross-Linking Strategy:
- Foundation articles (frameworks) anchor everything
- Deep-dive articles link back to foundations
- Quick insights serve as entry points
- Consistent terminology defined in systems-thinking-primer.md
